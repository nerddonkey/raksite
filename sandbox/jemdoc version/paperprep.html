the wrongs of righting

  • “that is” in the latin abbreviation way is written “, i.e., ”

  • non-informative comparisons to more informative comparisons

    • we get a result and compare it to previous work – says nothing

    • we get a result and show it is better than previous work – says nothing also because it might be a trivial improvement

    • we get a result and show it is significantly better than previous work – better because it asserts more than a trivial improvement, but it is still only qualitative

    • we get a result and show it is 3dB better than previous work – both qualitative and quantitative
